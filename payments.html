How to pay in the M‑Pesa App

  1. Open the M‑Pesa app.
  2. Tap Lipa na M‑PESA → Buy Goods and Services.
  3. Enter the Till Number: 819589.
  4. Enter the Amount we quoted/your order total.
  5. Confirm details and Pay.
  6. Save the confirmation message and share it on WhatsApp if requested.

How to pay via USSD (*334#)

  1. Dial *334# on your Safaricom line.
  2. Select Lipa na M‑PESA → Buy Goods and Services.
  3. Enter Till Number: 819589.
  4. Enter the Amount and your M‑Pesa PIN.
  5. Wait for the SMS confirmation.

After Payment

  • Send the confirmation text or screenshot to our WhatsApp (+254 700 592 619) with your Name & Order/Booking Ref.
  • We will verify and start your job immediately.

Tips
